Essential Info

Arrival Information

  • Check-in: 3:00 PM
  • Check-out: 11:00 AM
  • Parking: Self-parking in the underground garage is CA$15 per day and valet-parking is CA$50 per day.
  • Airport Shuttle: Our complimentary airport shuttles leave every hour on the hour, and make trips to Manila International Airport. Shuttles to Toronto Pearson are available twice a day for CA$50.
  • Trains: The nearest train station is at Manila Station, which is a major interchange for Via Rail trains running from Toronto west to Sarnia and Windsor.
  • Pet Policy: Pets of all sizes and types are allowed in designated pet rooms, and the specified common areas. Service animals are allowed everywhere.
